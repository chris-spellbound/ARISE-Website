Get Started

Jump to the AR Targets

Get up and explore an underwater world teeming with life!

Great storms destroyed the great underwater city of The Reef. It is up to you to befriend and reunite its inhabitants, and to rebuild the city to its former glory.

Beyond just being a game, ARISE is a medical tool used to get kids active and moving after surgery.

Learn more about using ARISE as a medical tool.

See the world in a new way with 11 AR targets to collect

ARISE is played on real-life targets (physical posters) that you can print and place all around you.

Find and meet dozens of friendly characters

Each friend you meet will grant you new missions, resources, and items to hunt for!

Beutifully crafted locations

Explore the beautiful world of the depths, all in AR.

  • Explore

    Visit places around your REAL world to make friends, gather resources, and find hidden treasures!

  • Build

    Design and rebuild your city completely from scratch!

  • Make New Friends!

    Go out and meet the animals of the reef! Each character has a unique story and personality!

  • Decorate!

    Our friends love to play dress up. Give them a fashionable style, or go crazy with it!
